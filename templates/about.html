How It Works

  • Enter Your Symptom: Tell Meditrain what you're feeling, and the AI will categorize your symptom.
  • Answer Follow-Up Questions: Based on your input, Meditrain will ask a few more questions to understand your condition better.
  • Get Your Diagnosis: After gathering enough information, you'll receive a possible condition, treatment suggestions, and common medications.
  • Improves Over Time: Each interaction helps Meditrain get smarter and offer more accurate advice.
